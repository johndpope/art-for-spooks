Using Art for Spooks

Hold the tablet over one of the images in the book, ensuring that the entire image is visible. Watch as the image changes before your eyes!

Note: tapping or swiping the images does not do anything.

Sharing

While viewing one of the images through the tablet, tap the "share" icon () to upload a screenshot to Twitter and Flickr, as well as posting to the Art for Spooks Facebook Page and the Art for Spooks Tumblr. The share icon only appears when viewing an image.
